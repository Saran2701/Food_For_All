About Us

Welcome to Food for All. Our initiative is to provide food to those in need and combat hunger across India. Join us in our journey to make a difference.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no one goes to bed hungry. We are committed to making this vision a reality through our various programs and initiatives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to collect excess food from marriage and party halls and provide it to those in need through the help of volunteers. This initiative aims to reduce food wastage and combat hunger.
